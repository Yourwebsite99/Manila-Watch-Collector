Our Standard

Forty-two checkpoints.
Zero shortcuts.

Every watch that passes through Manila Watch Collector is stripped down to fact: case authenticity, movement caliber, printing, lume application, and running accuracy — verified before it ever reaches a buyer.

  • Case & Bracelet Serial, link stamps, and finishing checked against factory spec.
  • Movement Caliber inspected and timed on a calibrated timegrapher.
  • Dial & Hands Printing, lume application, and font geometry cross-checked.
  • Papers Warranty card, box, and service history verified for continuity.
  • Pressure Test Water resistance confirmed before any sale is finalized.
